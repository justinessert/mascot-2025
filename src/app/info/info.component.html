🏀 About Mascot Madness Bracket

Welcome to Mascot Madness Bracket! This site lets you fill out a March Madness bracket **based on mascots instead of basketball performance**. Pick your favorite mascots and see how they stack up against others!

📖 How It Started

The origin of this project started when I was in high school and wanted to get my sister to create a march madness bracket. To convince her, we decided to create a shared bracket where we picked which mascot we liked the best. After a few years of moving Google Images windows around as we manually filled out a bracket, I decided to create some utilities to make this process easier. For the first couple years of this tool, it was something that could only run on my laptop, and was quite clunky to set up. After requests from other friends and family to get access to the tool, I decided to port it into a website. This website represents the latest iteration of that tool.

📌 How It Works

  • 🔹 Pick your favorite mascot in each matchup.
  • 🔹 Complete all rounds until you have a champion.
  • 🔹 Save your bracket and compete on the leaderboard.
